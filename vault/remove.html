sy vault remove --help

sy-vault-remove 
Delete a resource from the vault.

USAGE:
    sy vault --config-file <path> remove <path>...

FLAGS:
    -h, --help    Prints help information

ARGS:
    <path>...    The vault-relative path of a resource in the vault
